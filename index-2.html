Through our year long research, market analysis and many conducted focus groups we have managed to add our findings to our varied, collective 35 years of experience and knowledge of Elixir, Erlang and Clojure. From this we've concluded that offering 3 particular services will help all candidates to prosper in finding their next career opportunity.
